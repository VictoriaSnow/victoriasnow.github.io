Victoria Bian.

I'm Victoria Bian, a 20-yr old UX designer currently studying Cognitive Science, Computer Science and Design at UC Berkeley. Playing around with layouts and colors has been my biggest hobby for years. I just spent my summer with those awesome people at Gradescope and I am working with designers at Berkeley Innovation this semester to bring more simple and intuitive design solutions to real life problems.

As a designer, I explore all available resources to equip myself with better knowledge of design process as well as sharpen my skills in both experience and visual design. I am available for freelance this fall and I am also looking for internship opportunities for summer 2016.
