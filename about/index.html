Having fun with League of Legends

I used to think that LoL is nothing more than a time-consuming video game and I couldn't understand why people can go THAT crazy about eSports. However, since I started to play LoL for a couple of months, I began to realize that LoL is just like any other sports that requires strategy and speed and it's easily addicting because it's so fun. I end my day enjoying a couple of ARAMs or watching pro plays. My favorite champions to play are long-ranged mage and assassin.
