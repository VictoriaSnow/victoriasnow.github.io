I'm Victoria Bian. I think about the bigger picture to solve a problem. I combine the iterative brainstorming process with the pixel perfect craftsmanship to create a solution that solves people's needs.

I'm currently available for freelance design projects.

summer 2016 • ecommerce

I interned at Etsy in NYC as a product designer

As a product design intern, I worked closely with our team on designing and coding a delightful experience for sellers and buyers. I would like to share with you what I've learned! BTW, the project that I was part of was launched on Feb 14, 2017. Etsy Studio is Etsy's new marketplace for DIY dreamers and craft supply sellers: www.etsystudio.com.

spring 2016 • dev tool

I worked on the design for a freelance project called fossa

I worked with FOSSA to design a workflow for developers and product managers that streamlines the process of tracking and fixing issues related to licenses in code.

fall 2015 • productivity

I shaped the translation experience with waygo

Waygo is a real-time camera based translation app. As a side project for Berkeley Innovation, I worked in a group of designers to research the problems with the old user flow and redefine the experience for Waygo.

summer 2015 • education

I was a user interface design intern at gradescope

Gradescope is a start-up based in Berkeley that simplifies the grading process and providing analytical data to help instructors teach better. I helped craft the experience and visuals for online grading and brainstorm workflow for new features.

summer 2015 • entertainment

I designed chill for a better movie finding ux

Chill is an iOS app that provides you with customized movie recommendations catered to your own interests. My design focuses on simplifying the process of getting users' preferences as well as the way of presenting movie recommendations.
